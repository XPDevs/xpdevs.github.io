Announcement from XPDevs

Announcement from XPDevs: DoorsOS 4 is in Development! We at XPDevs are thrilled to officially announce that DoorsOS 4 is currently in development! Our team has been hard at work, and we are excited to share some early progress. So far, we have successfully developed the bootsect and the initial part of the installer. The boot process has been streamlined to deliver faster performance, and we've implemented a new file-checking mechanism that ensures smoother system start-ups. DoorsOS 4 will be powered by our custom-built kernel, NexShell, which serves as the foundation for the OS. NexShell is designed to optimize both hardware and software performance, delivering a highly efficient and responsive experience. It will not only improve system stability but also bring new capabilities that enhance the way users interact with their machines. In addition to a new kernel, the installer has been designed for simplicity and ease of use. So far, it includes essential functions like setting up passwords, agreeing to licenses, and ensuring a smooth installation process. More features are being developed, and the OS is shaping up to meet the expectations of both experienced and everyday users. Stay tuned for more updates as development progresses, and get ready to experience a new era of simplicity, power, and efficiency with DoorsOS 4, coming soon from XPDevs!
