About DoorsOS

DoorsOS is XPDevs’ flagship operating system, developed from the ground up to redefine simplicity, speed, and accessibility in computing. It is designed for users of all experience levels — from students and hobbyists to developers and professionals.

Unlike traditional systems, DoorsOS eliminates unnecessary bloat and focuses on performance, clarity, and control. Each build is optimised to run smoothly on low-end hardware while still delivering a full-featured experience, with a minimal footprint and clean interface.

With the transition to a bootable ISO format powered by our custom-built kernel, NexShell, DoorsOS has entered a new era of development. The system supports native shell commands, custom programs, and low-level enhancements — all developed in-house.

At XPDevs, we believe in empowering users through innovation. DoorsOS reflects that mission: efficient, open, and evolving — software built for real use, not just for show.

DoorsOS isn’t just an alternative OS — it’s a statement. Join us in building something smarter.
