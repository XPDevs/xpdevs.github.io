Announcement from XPDevs

At XPDevs, we are excited to announce a major overhaul of DoorsOS.

We have decided to completely refound the Doors Operating System. This means that the old batch file–based versions will no longer be developed, and future releases will focus solely on bootable ISO versions.

Additionally, DoorsOS® (powered by NexShell) will adopt a new versioning system starting from version 1. Technically, this new version 1 will be the fourth iteration of DoorsOS® (powered by NexShell), marking a fresh start for the OS.

We appreciate your continued support as we work towards making DoorsOS more powerful and accessible than ever.
